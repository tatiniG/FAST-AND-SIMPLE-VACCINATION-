SYMPTOMS

Understand the Symptoms

Fever

Fever is a common symptom of COVID-19. This is part of the body’s immune response to infection. If a person has a fever of 100.4°F (38°C) or above, they should stay at home and get medical advice as it is early symptom of COVID-19.

Dry Cough And Cold

Runny or stuffy nose and cough causes irritation and throat soreness, resulting in difficulty in breathing as well as swallowing food.

Loss of Taste And Smell

primary cause, particularly for people with extended or permanent loss of smell function, is that the virus causes an inflammatory reaction inside the nose that can lead to a loss of the olfactory, or smell, neurons.

PRECAUTIONS

Safety Is our No1 Priority

Wear Mask

Wear a mask when physical distancing is not possible. Don’t touch your eyes, nose or mouth.

Clean your hands.

Clean your hands often. Use soap and water, or an alcohol-based hand rub.

Maintain safe distance.

Maintain a safe distance from anyone who is coughing or sneezing.Stay home if you feel unwell.

Get vaccinated.
